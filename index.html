AMM CONTRACTING CORPORATION


Our way is “Honesty is the fastest way to prevent a mistake from turning into a failure. AMM CONTRACTING CORPORATION is a company that focuses on installation of (Air-conditioning, Ventilation, Fire protection systems, Plumbing Sanitary , Electrical and Renewable Energy Solar Panels, Solar Thermal Water, Heat pumps ) also we cater Pressurize Pipe installation and Fabrication. It is founded by three individuals in 2019 who specializes in the field of mechanical engineering and provides high quality services throughout the design, installation and construction process of the project. The company has a good track experience on working on industrial, commercial, and fit out constructions. The team focuses on advance construction methods, safety protocols, and company management to provide a smooth and steady flow of the work done. The team builds up long term relationship to our clients producing and developing highly efficient projects. Our aim is to one of the top leading company in the industry providing honesty and high quality of service to our clients. We take pride in the completion of every project producing high quality services to build our name to be recognized as one of the best and trustworthy.
